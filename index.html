Overview

My name is Noah Sternberg, and I am a senior at Murray State University, studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I was born in Evansville, Indiana, and moved to Paducah, Kentucky shortly there after, where I stayed my whole life, until moving to Murray, Kentucky for college. When starting college, I was a Mathematics major with an undeclared minor, before declaring my minor as Computer Science, until I realised computer science is what I want to do with my life, when I switched to a Computer Science major and Mathematics minor.

When I am not doing work for classes or my job at the time, I tend to find myself reading or playing video games. I also frequently listen to music, and work on some personal projects, like this website for example. You can find more info about what I am up to, like the book I'm reading, the show I'm watching etc on the "What am I ____" page.

In terms of my programming interests, I quite enjoy programming langauge development and compiler theory, as well as DevOps development. I have experience making RESTful APIs, as well as DevOps software, and modifying front end code as well. For more information on any of that, feel free to check out Education or Work, and to see my current projects pertaining to my interests, feel free to see Projects.

Details

I joined HCLTech in May of 2023 for my Summer internship, and I couldn't have enjoyed my time there more. I joined a team of 6 people, though my contacts were limited (for the most part) to my supervisor Rick, my mentors Koby and Jeff, and the person who reported my primary work item, Brian.

While I worked there, I tackled 4 things. First was a sample project to help me get familiar with the product I was working on, which was a DevOps product aimed at code deployments. Then I worked on a ticket to change something in the front end of the code. The main thing I worked on, however, was an internal tool to help prevent developers from putting database changes into a previous version of the product, which causes issues down the road. I also got started on another ticket that dealt with a procedure that can be ran within the app, but that didn't get done in the time I worked there.

While I worked there, we had daily scrum meetings, I had a weekly one on one meeting with my boss, and we also had regular design meetings, as well as retro and planning (standard agile meetings). I worked using the Java language, and the Dojo front end framework, as well as the Ant build system, and, of course, Git.

Made by me, for you. Thanks for checking this out.
